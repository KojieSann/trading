Brunei Darussalam

A small, wealthy nation located on the island of Borneo in Southeast Asia. It is bordered by the South China Sea and surrounded by the Malaysian state of Sarawak. Known for its lush rainforests, rich Islamic heritage, and high standard of living, Brunei is a peaceful country led by a monarchy. The current ruler, Sultan Hassanal Bolkiah, is one of the world's longest-reigning monarchs and heads both the state and the government.

Brunei's economy is heavily supported by oil and gas exports, which have allowed the country to provide free healthcare, education, and subsidized housing to its citizens. The capital city, Bandar Seri Begawan, is home to stunning architecture, including the famous Sultan Omar Ali Saifuddien Mosque and the opulent Istana Nurul Iman — the Sultan’s palace.

Brunei

International Airport

Located near Bandar Seri Begawan in the Brunei-Muara District, Brunei International Airport serves as the main gateway for air travel in the country. It is the home base for Royal Brunei Airlines and accommodates international flights across Asia, Oceania, the Middle East, and London Heathrow.

The airport features a passenger terminal with a capacity of up to three million passengers and includes amenities such as various dining options and retail stores. Additionally, it houses the Royal Brunei Air Force Base within its vicinity.

Muara

Port

Situated on the northeastern coast at the entrance of Brunei Bay, Muara Port is the largest and main international seaport of Brunei. Established in 1973 as a deep-water port, it serves as the primary gateway for the nation's trade. Managed by Muara Port Company Sdn Bhd—a joint venture between Darussalam Assets Sdn Bhd and China's Guangxi Beibu Gulf International Port Group—the port is undergoing expansion plans aimed at increasing its capacity and enhancing its role in regional trade within the BIMP-EAGA (Brunei Darussalam-Indonesia-Malaysia-Philippines East ASEAN Growth Area) region.

Bank Islam Brunei Darussalam (BIBD)
BIBD is the largest Islamic bank in Brunei and plays a central role in the country’s financial system. It offers a wide range of Shariah-compliant banking products and services, including personal and corporate banking, investment solutions, and digital banking services. BIBD is known for integrating Islamic values into its financial practices, aiming to promote ethical banking and sustainable economic growth in line with Brunei’s Islamic principles. Its strong local presence and commitment to innovation have made it a key player in the nation’s financial landscape.
